Ministry of Gender,Labour and Social Development

Complaints External Employment Management Information System (CEEMIS)

The Complaints External Employment Management Information System (CEEMIS) is a digital platform designed to streamline the reporting, management, and monitoring of employment-related complaints, particularly those affecting migrant workers and employment agencies. It enables multiple stakeholders, including government agencies, case managers, and complainants, to report, track, and resolve complaints efficiently.

Key Features:

  • Complaint Reporting: Submit complaints related to employment issues, including abuse, exploitation, or contractual disputes.
  • Case Management: Manage complaints, assign tasks, and track progress in real time.
  • Status Tracking: Monitor the status of cases from submission to resolution.
  • Multi-Agency Collaboration: Coordinate handling of cases across relevant agencies.
  • Data Analytics & Reporting: Gain insights into trends, recurring issues, and system performance.
  • Integration: Links with other government systems for verification and follow-up actions.
  • Mobile & Web Access: Convenient access for users on both web and mobile platforms.

Objective: CEEMIS improves transparency, reduces delays in complaint resolution, and empowers users by giving them a structured and accountable channel to report and follow up on employment-related grievances.
